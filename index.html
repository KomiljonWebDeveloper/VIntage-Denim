    ABOUT THE COMPANY

    Vintage Denim LLC was established in 2019 and is one of the leading textile factories in Uzbekistan. To this day, the company is engaged in the production of jeans, representing Uzbekistan in the world market.
    The company has its own brewery, cutting and sewing enterprises equipped with specialized sewing equipment of European and Japanese production (Brother, Brotders, Tajima, JUKI), produces world-class denim clothing in industrial batches.
